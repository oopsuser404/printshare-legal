Common Questions

How do I place an order?
Upload your STL file, choose a nearby printer based on price, distance, and rating, select your material and color, and pay securely through the app.

How do I get paid as a printer?
Set up payouts through Stripe in the app. When an order closes, your earnings transfer to your Stripe account. You can then withdraw to your bank.

What if there's a problem with my order?
Tap "Get Help" on your order detail screen. Your payment is held until the issue is resolved.

How do I cancel an order?
Pending orders can be cancelled for a full refund. Once a printer has accepted the job, contact support for assistance.
