Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Makewave ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Makewave mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App. By creating an account, accessing, or using Makewave, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, do not use Makewave.

1. Information we collect

When you create an account and use Makewave, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, profile name, and username you provide at signup.
  • Location data: your city, state, ZIP code, and approximate geographic coordinates to display nearby printers. We use the location you provide — we do not track your location in the background or when the App is not in use.
  • Order information: uploaded 3D model files (STL format), order details, delivery addresses, pricing information, order status history, ratings, and reviews.
  • Payment information: all payments are processed exclusively by Stripe, Inc. We do not store, process, or have access to your full credit card number, bank account number, or other sensitive financial account details on our servers. Stripe handles all payment data subject to their privacy policy.
  • Communications: chat messages exchanged between Requesters and Print Providers within the App, and any communications you send to us.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, push notification tokens, and unique device identifiers used to deliver order updates and notifications.
  • Usage data: information about how you interact with the App, including pages viewed, features used, actions taken, timestamps, and session duration.
  • Internet and network information: IP address, browser type (if accessing web-based components), and general network information collected automatically when you use the App.

2. Cookies and tracking technologies

Makewave and its service providers may use cookies, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve the App. These technologies may be used for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: to recognize you when you log in and maintain your session.
  • Preferences: to remember your settings and preferences across sessions.
  • Analytics and performance: to understand how Users interact with the App, identify errors, and improve functionality and performance.
  • Security: to detect and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, and other security threats.

We may update the tracking technologies we use as the App evolves. You may be able to control certain cookies and tracking technologies through your device or browser settings, but disabling them may affect the functionality of the App.

3. Analytics and usage tracking

We may use third-party analytics services to collect and analyze usage data about the App. These analytics providers may collect information such as your IP address, device type, operating system, App usage patterns, feature interactions, session duration, and crash reports. This data is collected in aggregate or pseudonymous form and is used solely to understand how Users interact with the App, diagnose technical issues, and improve the service. We do not use analytics data to personally identify individual Users for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and provide the Makewave marketplace service.
  • Match Requesters with nearby printer Print Providers based on location.
  • Process and facilitate orders and payments between parties.
  • Send push notifications about order status changes, new messages, ratings, and other relevant updates.
  • Calculate pricing, applicable taxes, and shipping costs.
  • Investigate and resolve disputes, support requests, and order holds.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect against fraud, abuse, or illegal activity.
  • Improve the App, develop new features, and analyze usage patterns.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including tax reporting requirements.
  • Communicate with you about your account, orders, and service-related matters.

5. Automated file processing

When you upload a 3D model file (such as an STL file) to Makewave, the file may be automatically processed by our systems for the purposes of calculating pricing estimates, estimating print time, determining material usage, analyzing file dimensions, detecting file errors, or performing file repair. This automated processing is conducted solely to provide and improve the service. No human review of uploaded file content is performed unless required for dispute resolution. We do not claim Print Providership of any uploaded files. Uploaded files are subject to the limited license described in our Terms of Service.

6. How we share your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing or advertising purposes. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Between transaction parties: when you place or fulfill an order, the other party may see your profile name, username, rating, review count, and delivery address (for shipping orders). Print Providers see the Requester's shipping address only when necessary to fulfill a shipping order.
  • Stripe: payment, payout, and identity verification information is shared with Stripe, Inc. to process transactions and facilitate payouts. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Service providers: we may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, database management, analytics, and push notification delivery. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us and in compliance with this policy.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect our rights, your safety, the safety of others, or to investigate fraud or respond to a security incident.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, reorganization, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in Print Providership or control of your personal information.

7. User-generated content visibility

Certain information you provide or generate through your use of Makewave may be visible to other Users or the public. This includes:

  • Profile information: your profile name, username, and star ratings are visible to other Users when they browse printers, view order details, or interact with you on the platform.
  • Ratings and reviews: your average rating and review count are displayed on your profile and may be visible to all authenticated Users of the App.
  • Printer listings: if you are an Print Provider, your printer listing information (printer model, available materials, colors, and general location) is visible to Requesters browsing the marketplace.
  • Transaction information: during an active order, the other party to the transaction may see your profile name, rating, and (for shipping orders) your delivery address. Delivery addresses are shared only with the Print Provider fulfilling the order and only for the purpose of shipping.

You should not include sensitive personal information in any user-generated content, including profile names, chat messages, or order notes.

8. Data storage and international transfers

Makewave is operated from the United States. Your information is stored and processed on servers located in the United States, including those operated by our service providers (such as Supabase and Stripe). If you access Makewave from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence. By using Makewave, you explicitly consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and to any other country where our service providers operate. We may change our hosting infrastructure or add service providers in additional jurisdictions in the future, and your continued use of the App constitutes consent to such transfers.

10. Data security and breach response

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Row-level security policies on all database tables.
  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL) for all communications between the App and our servers.
  • Payment processing handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.
  • Access controls limiting employee and contractor access to personal information on a need-to-know basis.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for any activity that occurs under your account.

In the event of a data breach or security incident that compromises your personal information, we will notify affected Users as required by applicable law. Notification may be provided via email, in-app notification, or other reasonable means. We will also notify relevant regulatory authorities as required. We will not, however, assume liability for breaches caused by factors beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to third-party service provider breaches, User credential compromise, or unauthorized access resulting from User negligence.

11. "Do Not Track" signals

Some web browsers transmit "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals to websites and applications. There is currently no universally accepted standard for how companies should respond to DNT signals. At this time, Makewave does not respond to or alter its data collection practices upon receiving DNT signals from your browser or device. This policy applies regardless of whether a DNT signal is received. If a uniform standard for responding to DNT signals is adopted in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.
